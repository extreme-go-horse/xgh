# xgh Cross-Team Pollinator

Promote useful patterns between teams without copying irrelevant local details.

## Workflow

1. Identify high-signal patterns from one team context tree.
2. Translate them into neutral guidance for cross-team adoption.
3. Preserve original source links and constraints.
4. Curate cross-team learnings for discoverability.

This cross-team process improves reuse while avoiding blind copy-paste.
